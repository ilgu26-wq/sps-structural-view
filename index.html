WHY THIS BRAIN GROWS BUT NEVER LEARNS FROM ACTION
This system is not reinforcement learning. It does not optimize a reward function. The judgment core (Layer 3) is frozen — it was designed once and sealed with a hash. No trade outcome, no PnL, no win/loss ratio can cross the irreversible field back into judgment.

Growth happens only in one place: the BetaPosterior (57 bins). And even there, growth means "narrowing confidence intervals" — not changing rules. As more trades are observed, each market-state bin becomes more certain about its classification. But the classification criteria themselves never change.

This is growth by accumulation, not modification. The structure is frozen. Only evidence moves.
