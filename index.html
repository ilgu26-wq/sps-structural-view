MEDS
Minimal Explanation Decision System
A decision system whose actions are invariant under explanation collapse.
Section 1
What Problem MEDS Solves
Most AI systems try to improve decisions by changing them. They learn from rewards, feedback, or outcomes.
This creates three structural failures:
  • Reward hacking
  • Decision drift
  • Silent instability
As explanations, memory, or rewards grow, the decision itself becomes unstable.
MEDS starts from the opposite assumption:
Decisions must be frozen.
Only explanations are allowed to grow.

In MEDS
